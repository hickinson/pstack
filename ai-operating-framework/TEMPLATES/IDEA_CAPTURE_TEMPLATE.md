# Idea Capture Template

## Idea title

[Insert title]

## Raw idea

What is the idea in plain language?

## Why it matters

Why does this feel worth exploring?

## Who it is for

Who would benefit if this worked?

## Signal or trigger

What prompted the idea?
Examples:
- repeated pain point
- opportunity spotted
- conversation
- article or post
- personal reflection

## First useful version

What would a small but meaningful first version look like?

## Constraints or realities

What obvious limits already apply?

## Open questions

What is still unclear?

## Hand-off note

What should the next step do?
Examples:
- clarify the task
- research the space
- create a plan
