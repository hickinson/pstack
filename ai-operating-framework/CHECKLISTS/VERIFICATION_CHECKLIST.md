# Verification Checklist

Verify the output using checks proportionate to the task.

## Verification questions
- What exactly am I trying to prove?
- What is the right validation method?
- What was actually tested or checked?
- What remains untested?
- What assumptions remain?
- Is the output ready despite those uncertainties?

## Evidence categories
Use one or more:
- logic check
- source check
- formatting check
- scenario walk-through
- peer review
- test execution
- policy check
- human sign-off

## Outcome
Choose one:
- verified enough to deliver
- partially verified, caveats required
- not verified enough, revise first
