> Use this to challenge the quality of an output before calling it complete.
# Review Checklist

Review the output against the following:

## Brief alignment
- Does it answer the actual objective?
- Has scope drift occurred?
- Are assumptions visible?

## Quality
- Is it coherent?
- Is it complete enough for its purpose?
- Is the level of detail right?
- Is anything obviously weak, vague, or contradictory?

## Practicality
- Can someone use this?
- Are next steps clear?
- Is it overcomplicated?

## Risk
- Does it touch sensitive judgement?
- Are caveats or limits visible?
- Should a human review be stronger here?

## Recommendation
Choose one:
- ready
- revise
- escalate
